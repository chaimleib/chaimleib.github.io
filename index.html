Where are the Seligson Zichronos?

I regret that I had to do it, but my host reduced the capabilities of my server, and it is no longer possible to host as much content as I used to. I have been hosting these zichronos for free for the past few years, and I found even then that I was running up against the limits of what the my host's server could do. I was technically unable to continue adding content.

Then, in mid-2023, things suddenly got worse. I tried to deploy a tiny text-only update, and the deploy failed because processing all the audio took too much time.

A hard decision had to be made: What content to remove? The two most resource-intensive parts of my site were the Schapiro Zichronos and the Seligson Zichronos. I decided to remove the Seligson Zichronos because it was a less-polished part of my site. I may have to remove the Schapiro Zichronos as well.

In case things change, I have saved the data in the pre-zichronos-cut branch on my GitHub repository for this site. I hope someday the zichronos will return and be updated better than before.

I apologize for the sudden change.
